Embassy of Hope

The Embassy of Hope in Hamburg is a community initiative hosted at the Thalia Theater. It serves as a cultural and social hub, offering various activities and support services primarily aimed at refugees and migrants.

Yalla Hamburg

Yalla Hamburg is an initiative aimed at helping newcomers, especially refugees, to integrate into Hamburg's community by participating in free or low-cost activities across the city.

AGIJ e.V.

The Arbeitsgemeinschaft Interkultureller Jugendverbände (AGIJ e.V.) in Hamburg is an umbrella organization for intercultural youth associations.

Mitmacher

MitMacher in Hamburg is an initiative that focuses on integrating refugees and migrants into voluntary work.​

Sprachbrücke

Sprachbrücke-Hamburg e.V. is an organization in Hamburg dedicated to helping adult immigrants improve their German language skills through conversation practice.
